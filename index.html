Xspect Inc.

Xspect Inc. is a company specializing in acceptance testing and accreditation inspections of diagnostic radiology equipment. We provide Mammography Accreditation “Physicist” inspections for the Ontario Breast Screening Program and for the Canadian Association of Radiologists Mammography Accreditation Program. We provide BMD Accreditation “Physicist” inspections for the Canadian Bone Mineral Densitometry Accreditation Program. We also provide Computed Tomography Acceptance Tests for hospitals and clinics installing new CT scanners.

As a physics support company we also provide "shielding calculations" for X-ray rooms, lead thickness determinations for existing operator viewing windows, Safety Code 35 inspections, and other unique project needs which may occur.

We are also offering a Quality Improvement and Support Program to help departments optimise their images within the digital dose/quality envelope.

We currently have two full time physicists on staff, and we may supplement this talent on occasion using our connections in this field.
